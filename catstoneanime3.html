What is Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is a popular action and dark fantasy anime created by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose peaceful life is destroyed when demons attack his family. His younger sister, Nezuko Kamado, survives but is turned into a demon. Determined to save her and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, a secret organization that protects humanity from deadly demons.

As Tanjiro trains and grows stronger, he goes on dangerous missions and meets loyal friends like Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira. The anime is famous for its breathtaking animation, emotional storytelling, and intense sword fights using powerful breathing techniques. Demon Slayer teaches powerful lessons about family, courage, and never giving up even in the darkest times.
