👹 What is Chainsaw Man?

🪚 Chainsaw Man is a dark action anime created by Tatsuki Fujimoto. The story follows Denji, a poor young man who works as a devil hunter to pay off his father’s debt.

🔥 After a tragic betrayal, Denji merges with his pet devil Pochita and gains the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws. He joins a government devil-hunting organization and fights powerful devils while chasing simple dreams like living a normal life.
