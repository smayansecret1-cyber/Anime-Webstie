🦸 What is One Punch Man?

💥 One Punch Man is an action-comedy anime created by ONE. The story follows Saitama, a hero who has become so strong that he can defeat any opponent with a single punch.

🦸‍♂️ Although incredibly powerful, Saitama feels bored because no enemy can challenge him. Alongside his disciple Genos, he joins the Hero Association and fights powerful monsters. The anime is famous for its incredible animation, hilarious comedy, and epic battle scenes.
